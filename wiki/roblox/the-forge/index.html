The Forge

The Forge is a Roblox game released on November 25th, 2025. Gameplay includes:

  • Mining ore veins in unstable caverns
  • Forging materials into weapons and armor
  • Combat with zombies and subterranean enemies
  • Exploration of high-risk regions and hidden areas
  • Players use forged and purchasable gear such as pickaxes and weapons to survive locations like Stonewake's Cross, the Forgotten Kingdom, and the Goblin Cave.
